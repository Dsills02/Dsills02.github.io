Date Caramel

Ingredients

  • 20 pitted Medjool dates
  • 2/3 to 3/4 cup hot oat milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • pinch of sea salt , optional

Instructions

  1. Soak the Dates Place the pitted dates in a bowl and cover them with hot water. Let Them Soak for about an hour 15-20 minutes. This will soften them , making it easier to blend into a smooth Caramel

  2. Drain the Dates After soaking , drain the dates of the water.
